Dory - Your Personal Health Companion App

Dory is the one app that you will ever need to live a healthy life.Patients and Doctors can view all the
previous medical history.The assistant inbuilt in the app can go through the prescription and remind users
to take their medicine at the required times.It can also assess people's mental state by the means of data
collected from smart wearables and also in the means of a chat bot.Users can also directly connect with
their doctors thtough the connect section and also book an appointment from within the app
